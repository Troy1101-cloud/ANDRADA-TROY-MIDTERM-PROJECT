The Cinema Room

An Exhibition of My Favorite Films

Avengers: Endgame
2019
Directors: Anthony Russo, Joe Russo
Cast: Robert Downey Jr., Chris Evans, Scarlett Johansson
After the devastating events of Infinity War, the Avengers assemble once more to help reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe.
Thor: Ragnarok
2017
Director: Taika Waititi
Cast: Chris Hemsworth, Cate Blanchett, Mark Ruffalo
Imprisoned on the other side of the universe, Thor must escape and race against time to prevent the destruction of his home world and the end of all civilization on Asgard.
How to Train Your Dragon
2010
Directors: Dean DeBlois, Chris Sanders
Cast: Jay Baruchel, America Ferrera
A young Viking reluctant to kill dragons befriends and flies a dangerous dragon's offspring, challenging his society's traditions and bridging two worlds through an unlikely friendship.
Demon Slayer: Mugen Train
2020
Director: Haruo Sotozaki
Cast: Natsuki Hanae, Daiki Yamashita
Tanjiro and his companions face off against a powerful Upper Moon Demon aboard the Mugen Train, pushing their abilities to the limit in this emotional and action-packed adventure.
One Piece Film: Gold
2016
Director: Hiroaki Miyamoto
Cast: Mayumi Tanaka, Akemi Okamura
Luffy and his crew embark on a thrilling adventure in Gran Tesoro, a massive floating city made of gold, where they face treacherous challenges and a formidable enemy seeking revenge.
The Conjuring
2013
Director: James Wan
Cast: Vera Farmiga, Patrick Wilson, Lili Taylor
Paranormal investigators work to help a family plagued by a dark spirit in their farmhouse. A terrifying encounter based on true events that will keep you on the edge of your seat.
Hereditary
2018
Director: Ari Aster
Cast: Toni Collette, Gabriel Byrne
When the matriarch of a family dies, her children begin experiencing terrifying supernatural phenomena. A psychological horror masterpiece exploring family trauma and dark secrets.
