“~GROWING TOGETHER WITH YOU~”

How It Shaped My College Experience

College has been one of the most challenging chapters ng buhay ko ngayon, and I'm only at my second year. Sa dami-dami ng quiz, deadlines, at moments na kinukwestyon ko ang sarili ko, having a relationship has helped me a lot kung paano ko na-maintain ang studies ko.

Hindi madali ang lahat, pero malaking tulong ang pagkakaroon ng taong nakakaintindi at sumusuporta sa akin habang nag-aaral pa ako. In this blog, I'll share how growing together with my partner is helping me navigate my college journey academically, emotionally, and personally.

I. Growing Through Mutual Support

One thing I appreciate most about my relationship is that we grow together. Pareho kaming may pangarap at pareho kaming dumadaan sa hirap ng pagiging estudyante. Instead of competing with each other, we motivate each other. Kapag nawawalan ako ng gana mag-aral, siya yung nagpapaalala na pansamantala lang ang hirap at may mas magandang future na naghihintay.

There were times na sabay kaming nag-aaral kahit hindi pareho ang subjects. Kahit simpleng presence lang, malaking bagay na. It made me realize that growth doesn't always have to be individual. Minsan, mas nagiging madali ang pag-unlad kapag may kasama kang handang umunawa at sumuporta.

II. Finding Balance Between School and Relationships

Hindi rin madali ang mag-balance ng relationship at acads. May moments na kailangan kong unahin ang school, at may times din na kailangan kong magbigay ng oras sa relationship. Through communication and understanding, natutunan naming irespeto ang priorities ng isa't isa.

Imbes na maging distraction, naging motivation ang relationship ko. Kapag tapos na ang tasks ko, mas nagiging rewarding ang pahinga. Natutunan kong maging mas responsible sa oras ko dahil ayokong mapabayaan pareho ang studies at ang relationship.

III. Overcoming Challenges and Building Strength

Syempre, hindi perpekto ang lahat. May misunderstandings, tampuhan, at moments na pareho kaming stressed. Pero doon ko nakita ang strength ng relationship namin. Instead of giving up, we choose to talk things out. Natutunan kong maging mas patient, mas understanding, at mas emotionally mature.

These challenges taught me lessons that I also apply in school—handling pressure, accepting criticism, and communicating properly. What I learn in my relationship helps me grow not just as a partner, but also as a student and as a person.

Lessons Beyond the Classroom

Looking back, I can say na malaking bahagi ng college journey ko ang relationship ko. It helped me emotionally, mentally, and even academically. Hindi man nito inalis ang hirap ng college, pero binigyan ako nito ng lakas para ipagpatuloy ang laban.

My biggest takeaway is that having the right person beside you can make a big difference. Hindi kailangan perpekto ang relasyon—basta may respeto, suporta, at pang-unawa. Moving forward, I plan to keep prioritizing my studies while nurturing a healthy relationship. College is temporary, but the lessons I learn from both school and love will stay with me for life.
