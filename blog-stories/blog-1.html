How AI Helped Me Through My College Journey

HOW AI HELPED ME

Honestly speaking, AI changed the game big time for me as an IT student because it completely transformed how I approach learning and problem-solving. Dati, akala ko AI is purely sci-fi and pang movie lang—robots, advanced machines, and ideas that felt too far from real life. But now, it has become part of my daily academic routine.

Ginagamit ko na ito for writing, debugging programs, doing research, and even brainstorming ideas whenever I feel stuck or overwhelmed with schoolwork. Nakaka-amaze siya dahil sobrang helpful and time-saving, pero at the same time nakakatakot dahil napapaisip ako kung minsan kung nagiging too dependent na ba ako sa paggamit ng AI instead of relying on my own skills.

AI AS MY TUTOR

Dahil ngayong isa na akong IT student, AI helped me a lot—it became my tutor. It helps me understand difficult programming concepts or yung mga tinuturo ng mga prof na hindi ko agad nage-gets. Kapag may hindi ako maintindihan sa code or logic, I always ask AI, and it explains everything to me using sample programs and step-by-step guides.

Oo, hindi siya laging on point, pero it serves as a starter guide that gives me direction para mas maintindihan ang lessons ng mga professor ko. Dahil dito, mas nagiging confident ako mag-aral at mas naiintindihan ko na ang mga topic na dati ay nahihirapan ako.

School and Daily Life Works

AI tools help organize my life by making tasks faster and more efficient. Platforms like Canva use AI to help me create layouts and presentations for projects. Google Drive provides storage and gives suggestions that improve my documents and presentations. Even on social media, AI is everywhere—YouTube recommends tutorials, TikTok shares productivity tips, and Instagram entertains me with memes and trends based on my interests. Messaging apps like Messenger also use AI to filter spam and unwanted messages, making communication safer and more convenient.

Fear vs Advantage

Pero may patuloy na debate na nagsasabing magiging tamad daw ang students dahil sa sobrang pagdepende sa AI. Para sa akin, nakadepende pa rin ito sa tamang paggamit. AI can be a learning tool that explains difficult concepts, or a shortcut that kills effort when abused.

AI can speed up tasks, but it cannot replace critical thinking, logical reasoning, and problem-solving skills lalo na para sa IT students. In my experience, mas natuto pa nga akong mag-debug dahil ine-explain ng AI ang errors in simple language instead of just giving answers.

The Future of Technology

Sa huli, malinaw sa akin na ang AI ay hindi kaaway kundi isang powerful tool na puwedeng makatulong sa pag-aaral at pag-develop ng skills. Ang mahalaga ay gamitin ito bilang suporta at hindi pamalit sa sariling effort.

Through AI, I learned to adapt, think critically, and solve problems more effectively while still valuing my own skills and creativity. Bilang future tech professional, mahalaga na maging open-minded sa AI at handang gamitin ito sa mas advanced na paraan sa hinaharap.
