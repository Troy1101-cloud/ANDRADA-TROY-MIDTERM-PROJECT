Debugging Today, Building Tomorrow

Thinking About the Future While Still in College

Bilang isang 2nd Year BSIT student, mas madalas ko nang iniisip kung anong klaseng IT professional ang gusto kong maging pagkatapos ng college. I am not yet a graduate and I still have a lot to learn, pero habang tumatagal, mas nagiging malinaw sa akin na mahalagang magkaroon ng direksyon kahit maaga pa.

Sa lahat ng projects, coding activities, at challenges na hinaharap ko, college life is gradually preparing me for the real-world IT environment. This blog talks about the IT career I see myself building in the next five years and how my experiences as a second-year student are shaping my future.

Finding My Place in the IT World

At first, pinili ko ang IT dahil sa interest ko sa computers and technology, and because my dad is also in the IT field. As a 2nd year student, mas lumalim ang exposure ko sa programming, systems, at problem-solving.

I realized that IT is not just about coding, but about creating solutions for real-life problems. Kahit minsan mahirap at nakaka-pressure, natututo akong maging patient at mas critical sa pag-iisip.

The IT Career I Want to Pursue in the Next 5 Years

Sa loob ng susunod na limang taon, nakikita ko ang sarili ko na nagtatrabaho sa IT industry, posibleng bilang software developer or system-related professional. Hindi ko pa alam ang eksaktong role na babagsakan ko, pero alam kong gusto kong mapunta sa career na patuloy akong hinahamon at pinapalawak ang kaalaman ko. Ang goal ko ay magkaroon ng stable na IT job kung saan magagamit ko ang skills na hinuhubog ko pa lang ngayon.

What I’m Doing Now as a 2nd Year Student

Habang nasa 2nd year pa lang ako, sinisimulan ko nang ihanda ang sarili ko. I focus on understanding basic programming logic, system flow, at tamang approach sa debugging.

Hindi ko minamadali ang sarili ko na maging expert agad. Para sa akin, mas mahalaga ang strong foundation dahil ito ang magiging sandigan ko pagdating sa mas advanced na subjects at sa actual work environment.

Small Steps Toward a Bigger Goal

Sa huli, ang career na nakikita ko para sa sarili ko sa loob ng limang taon ay nagsisimula sa kung ano ang ginagawa ko ngayon bilang 2nd Year BSIT student. My personal takeaway is that progress doesn’t have to be fast—it just has to be consistent.

Hangga’t patuloy akong nag-aaral, nage-effort, at hindi sumusuko, naniniwala akong darating ako sa career na pinapangarap ko. College is just the beginning, and I’m still learning, but I’m moving forward with purpose and hope for the future.
